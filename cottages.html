OCEAN-FRONT COTTAGES

MacDuff’s Ocean Front Cottage is a duplex-style cottage featuring simple, cozy décor, simultaneously contemporary and comfortable. Guests need only step out the front door to enjoy three miles of pristine beachfront.

With 547 Sq. Ft. of spacious accommodations, the Ocean Front Cottage is ideal for a romantic getaway or some much-needed rest and relaxation. Each cottage is equipped with a king-sized bed, a private living room, and a covered porch that affords a spectacular view of the ocean, designed to feel more like home, rather than a typical suite.

OCEAN-VIEW COTTAGES

Imagine waking to the sound of the ocean, and not tourists or traffic. Step outside and look out on a lush garden, and beyond it, a strip of vacant white sandy beach and the gently rolling surf. The Ocean View Cottage, set slightly inland amid tropical trees and vegetation, gives every guest the perception of native island life.

Boasting 708 square feet, this cottage is perfect for families and couples traveling together. With a king-sized bed and two queen day beds, the Ocean View arrangements can easily accommodate four to six people. It’s the quintessential place to end a day of activities or relaxation on the beach.
