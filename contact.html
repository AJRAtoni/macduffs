norman's cay is located 55 miles southeast of nassau in the northern exuma islands of the bahamas


BY COMMERCIAL AIR

There are numerous daily non-stop commercial flights into Nassau’s Lynden Pindling International Airport (NAS) from major cities, including Miami, New York, Toronto and London. From Nassau Airport, we would be happy to assist in arranging your transfer to a charter flight direct to Norman’s Cay. The flight from one of Nassau’s executive airports to Norman’s Cay is a short 20 minutes.

Please contact fly@normanscayexumas.com for more information on booking a charter flight between Nassau and Norman’s Cay.

BY CHARTER AIR

Perhaps the fastest way to enjoy a weekend getaway is to charter your own private flight from Opa-Locka to Norman’s Cay, depending on your schedule and their availability. The trip takes approximately one hour and forty-five minutes, including a stop in Fresh Creek, Andros.

To book a flight or for more information, please contact: fly@normanscayexumas.com

Please note that departure times are subject to change depending on the season. Passenger information, including a copy of your passport, must be provided at least 24 hours before the scheduled flight. Please contact fly@normanscayresort. com with inquiries or to submit documentation.
